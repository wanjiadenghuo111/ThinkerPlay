## Experiment Backlog

### Algorithms

- Sorting: bubble, insertion, merge, quick sort.
- Pathfinding: BFS, Dijkstra, A*.
- Recursion: tree growth, maze generation, fractal drawing.

### AI

- Neural network forward and backward propagation.
- Gradient descent landscape explorer.
- Genetic algorithm evolution.
- Decision tree splitting visualizer.

### Math

- Mandelbrot and Julia sets.
- Fourier drawing.
- Geometry transforms.
- Probability distribution sampler.

### Physics

- Particle system.
- Pendulum and spring simulation.
- Collision and conservation of momentum.
- Wave interference.

### Systems

- Conway's Game of Life.
- Boids flocking.
- Queueing system simulation.
- Cache replacement policies.

## Quality Bar

Before an experiment is marked as polished:

- It has a stable `index.html`.
- It has an experiment README.
- It has a preview asset or a clear visual entry on the project homepage.
- It works on a normal desktop browser.
- Its controls are understandable without reading source code.
